FAQ
How do I get appointed with Koop?
Please fill out the following web form, and a representative will get in touch. Alternatively, you can send us a message here for a quick response. Our appointment process is fast and easy.
What products can I start selling today?
Currently, we offer an industry-first integrated product for automation and robotics called the Singularity Package. It is a multi-line package consisting of Tech E&O, Cyber, and General Liability. We also offer these products on a monoline basis.
How can I unlock new classes of business?
With Koop, you can start writing new business in a matter of days. With our unique insurance products and platform features, you can market your brokerage services across the automation, robotics, and emerging technology space. Get appointed today or contact us to learn more!
Does Koop offer tools to help my customers?
Yes, our Singularity Platform enables customer-facing features for streamlined submissions, account management, and coverage servicing. If you have any technical difficulties or questions, our dedicated customer service team is ready to assist. Learn more about our technology here.
Where can I find more information about automation and robotics?
Online resources provide a good overview of these emerging industries. If you want to learn more, please feel free to get in touch. Additionally, we’re happy to work with you to develop co-branded marketing materials for your customer base.
