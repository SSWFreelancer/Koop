What is Tech E&O?

Technology Errors & Omissions is a type of liability coverage designed to protect your company from technical mistakes and erroneous system behavior. It covers products and services leading to third-party bodily injury, property damage, and operations interruption. For example, if you're a robotics company performing an inspection for a client, this coverage will protect you from robot malfunction causing property damage. If you're a software company providing  a system automation service, you will be covered if a software deficiency disrupts operations and results in financial loss. Tech E&O is fit for developers and operators with hardware- and software-defined exposure across multiple industries and use cases. Read More

This coverage is a must-have and often contractually required, which makes it essential for any business looking to scale operations in the modern world.

Example Use Cases

Sidewalk Delivery
Service

Exposed to third-party injury or property damage due to technical issues with robots. For example, if a delivery cart accidentally causes an auto accident.

Infrastructure
Inspection Service

Exposed to third-party property damage due to system malfunctions. For example, if a robot crashes into your client’s infrastructure and causes damage.

Automation
Orchestration Platform

Exposed to third-party operations interruption due to a software glitch. For example, if a software system causes loss of business for your clients.

Plug-n-Play
Autonomy Provider

Exposed to third-party bodily injury or property damage due to operational problems. For example, if an autonomy-equipped vehicle mishandles a task.

Drone-Based
Security Service

Exposed to third-party bodily injury or property damage due to accidental impact. For example, if an unmanned aerial vehicle falls onto private property or public roads.

Warehouse
Automation System

Exposed to third-party business interruption, property damage, or bodily injury due to downtime or damage. For example, if a system breaks down or catches fire.
